The E-Tendering Portal enables the Tenderers to download Tender Schedule  
 free of cost and then submit the bids online through this portal.

E-Tendering Portal vision is to add value to trading parties by facilitating their procurement process for both procurer and supplier. To improve the productivity and effectiveness for various business processes is Go-Business's desire in facilitating the trading amongst business partner. The efficiency of procurement process will be greatly improved by providing an integrated business solution. E-Tendering Portal represents the next generation of tendering solution. It will become one of the most convenient way of business opportunities search and information exchange amongst trading partners.

E-Tendering Portal is the innovative solution to procurement by applying latest technology in application development and Internet security. The business transaction will be processed under a secure environment and in a more efficient way. It is the newest and most cost effective way to perform tendering.
